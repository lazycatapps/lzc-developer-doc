# Contributors
The development of LCMD MicroServer cannot be separated from the support of our fans and experts. Thank you for your enthusiasm and sharing. You are amazing!

Below is the list of all contributors, in no particular order:

## glzjin
[glzjin](https://x.com/glzjin) is a security expert who has provided us with a lot of security defense suggestions and code suggestions. His daily encouragement is the driving force for our progress. He is also the author of the [lzc-dtl](https://github.com/glzjin/lzc-dtl) tool, which can convert Docker to LCMD MicroServer applications with one click, making it very convenient for developers to experiment with various fun applications and benefit the general public. Thank you, expert.

Through his automatic conversion tool, he has ported applications such as Xunlei, Certimate, Trilium, Cyberchef, RevShells, SQLi-Labs, Nut-WebGUI, Web-Chains, SuperShell, etc.

## rex
[rex](https://x.com/RetroCN_Shop) is an expert in both gaming and Linux. On the first day of receiving LCMD, he completed the porting of [Factorio](https://gitee.com/trexguo/lzc-factorio-server). Thank you for porting such a great game for us, allowing LCMD users to call a few good friends after work to play private server games together. Thank you, expert.

## IronFeet
[IronFeet](https://x.com/ironfeet) is a Linux and hardware hacker, and also the porting developer of the store AI application [AnythingLLM](https://lazycat.cloud/appstore/%2Fshop%2Fdetail%2Fme.ironfeet.app.anythingllm). AnythingLLM is a full-stack application that can convert any document, resource (such as URL links, audio, video) or content fragments into context for any large language model (LLM) to use as reference during chat. Thank you for your enthusiastic contribution.
